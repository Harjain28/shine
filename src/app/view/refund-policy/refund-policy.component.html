Refund Policy

    At CreditEnable, we are committed to providing valuable credit remediation services to Indian MSMEs. We understand that circumstances may arise where a refund is warranted. Please review our refund policy below:

  • 1. Tiered Assessment

    Our product offers three tiers, each unlocking a more nuanced level of assessment of the borrower's credit, banking, and GST profile.

    The highest tier provides actionable solutions, potential rates from our lenders, and improvements in probabilities based on suggested corrective actions.

  • 2. Refund Eligibility

    Customers are eligible for a refund in case we unable able to generate a report due to unavailability of data or technical issues.

    Please email your query to refunds@creditenable.com with a screenshot and we will process the same.

    Refunds will only be processed for requests that are raised within 7 days of making the payment and subject to the company not being able to resolve the same within 7 days and provide a report back to the user.

  • 3. Bureau History Requirement

    Customers without a bureau history are automatically eligible for a refund, as a bureau history is necessary to initiate a majority of the assessment process.

  • 4. How to Request a Refund

    To request a refund, please contact our customer support team at refunds@creditenable.com with a screenshot and we will process the same within 7 days.

    Please provide relevant details such as the reason for the refund and any specific issues faced.

    Depending on the type of payment method used, the refund will reflect in your account within 3-7 days.

  • 5. Refund Approval

    Refund requests will be reviewed promptly within 7 days.

    Refunds will be approved for eligible cases within a reasonable timeframe.

  • 6. Contact Information

    For refund inquiries, please reach out to our customer support team at refunds@creditenable.com.

  • Note: We reserve the right to amend this refund policy at our discretion.
